About

I'm hardworking and passionate about tech, just had an exciting 5-month internship at Beckster LTD. I joined the company in Autumn of 2020 where I revamped the company's store-front site, the new design increased engagement and reversed the bounce rate.

My web development journey began in 2014, back then I was pursuing a BTEC course in Business Management and Financial services. I met an IT tutor who was managing her own computer vending store at the college, it was at her store that I would get my laptop fixed and learned how to code. Later in the year she introduced me to a national competition called 'iDEA', an initiative that was in partnership with the Duke of York and the American Artist Will.IAM. 

The competition required students to develop an innovative solution-based idea, the prize was £10,000 for investment. Under the guidance of the tutor I created an overseas-qualifications converter to assist foreign students, including refugees, to find their way around academic choices easily.

Adobe DreamWeaver and Google slides were the tools I used in designing the mobile app. My design process would start in Google Slides where I did all the sketching and to bring the project to life I would use Adobe DreamWeaver. I was amongst the top finishers and got awarded a Digital bronze badge, left with brilliant skills and have since grown my network of contacts. My biggest  passion at the moment is sketching and building prototypes from the ground up.
