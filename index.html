About

A bit more about me..

I'm a cool guy who loves to code and workout in the gym, skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, UX and UI design, among other skills. Also I'm an experienced founder with a Business Management and Accounting and Finance degree from Nottingham Business School, meaning I can be the go to person for all things Business planning and strategy.

My web development journey began in 2014, back then I was pursuing a BTEC course in Business Management and Financial services. I met an IT tutor who was managing her own computer vending store at the college, it was at her store that I would get my laptop fixed and learned how to code. Later in the year she introduced me to a national competition called 'iDEA', an initiative that was in partnership with the Duke of York and the American Artist Will.IAM.

The competition required students to develop an innovative solution-based idea, the prize was £10,000 for investment. Under the guidance of the tutor I created an overseas-qualifications converter to assist foreign students, including refugees, to find their way around academic choices easily.

Adobe DreamWeaver and Google slides were the tools I used in designing the web app version. My design process would start in Google Slides where I did all the sketching and to bring the project to life I would use Adobe DreamWeaver. I was amongst the top finishers and got awarded a Digital bronze badge, left with brilliant skills and have since grown my network of contacts and web development knowledge. My biggest passion right now is User research, sketching and building prototypes from the ground up.
